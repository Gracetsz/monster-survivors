About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an intense survival action game where your goal is simple: stay alive as long as possible against increasingly difficult waves of monsters. Choose your character, collect power-ups, and fight your way through hordes of creatures that want nothing more than to end your run.

How to Play

Movement is automatic in Monster Survivors. Your character will continuously attack nearby enemies while you focus on navigating the dangerous battlefield. Collect experience gems to level up and choose new abilities or upgrade existing ones. The longer you survive, the stronger the monsters become!

Unique Characters

Choose from multiple characters, each with their own starting weapons and special abilities that dramatically change your gameplay experience.

Power-Up System

Collect a wide variety of weapons and passive items to create powerful synergies. Build your character's strength through strategic power-up choices.

Endless Challenge

Test your skills against increasingly difficult waves of monsters. How long can you survive in this challenging roguelike experience?

Game Tips

Focus on movement rather than attacking, as your character attacks automatically. Prioritize collecting experience gems to level up quickly. Don't get cornered by monsters - always leave yourself an escape route. Some weapon combinations are more effective than others, so experiment with different builds to find what works best for your playstyle.

System Requirements

Monster Survivors is a browser-based game that runs smoothly on most devices. For the best experience, we recommend using an updated browser like Chrome, Firefox, or Safari. The game supports both keyboard and touch controls, making it perfectly playable on desktop computers, tablets, and mobile phones.
