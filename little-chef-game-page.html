About Little Chef

Welcome to the charming world of Little Chef, where you'll step into the shoes of an aspiring culinary master! This fast-paced cooking game challenges your timing, precision, and multitasking abilities as you prepare various dishes against the ticking clock.

Chop vegetables, grill meats, and plate up delicious masterpieces to satisfy your demanding customers. With each successful dish, you'll earn points and unlock new recipes and kitchen tools to expand your culinary repertoire.

Whether you're a seasoned gamer or just looking for a fun way to pass the time, Little Chef offers an engaging experience that's easy to learn but challenging to master.

How to Play

  • 1.

    Click or tap on ingredients to select them.

  • 2.

    Drag ingredients to appropriate cooking stations (cutting board, grill, pot, etc.).

  • 3.

    Follow recipe instructions and timing carefully to avoid burning or undercooking food.

  • 4.

    Plate the finished dishes and serve them to customers before time runs out.

  • 5.

    Earn points for successful dishes and lose points for mistakes or slow service.
