How to Play

In Monster Survivors, your character attacks automatically, allowing you to focus entirely on movement and positioning. Navigate through hordes of enemies, collect experience gems to level up, and choose new abilities or upgrade existing ones to become more powerful.

Movement

Use WASD or arrow keys (desktop) or virtual joystick (mobile) to move your character around the battlefield. Positioning is crucial for survival!

Leveling

Collect the glowing experience gems dropped by defeated enemies to level up. Each level grants you a choice of three random power-ups.

Power-ups

Choose wisely between new weapons, weapon upgrades, or passive items that enhance your stats like speed, damage, or area of effect.

Strategy Tips

Success in Monster Survivors requires smart movement and power-up choices. Focus on building a cohesive set of abilities that complement each other rather than taking random upgrades. Keep moving to avoid being surrounded, and prioritize collecting experience gems to level up quickly.

Different enemies have varying behaviors and attack patterns. Learn to recognize dangerous monster types and prioritize them. Don't get cornered in tight spaces, and try to maintain a safe distance while your attacks do the work.
