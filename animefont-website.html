About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an action-packed survival game where you must face endless hordes of monsters. Collect power-ups, unlock new abilities, and see how long you can survive in this challenging roguelike experience.

How to Play

Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character around the map. Your character attacks automatically, so focus on positioning yourself strategically to defeat enemies while avoiding damage. Collect experience orbs to level up and choose new abilities or upgrade existing ones.

Endless Gameplay

Character Development

Level up your character, unlock new abilities, and customize your playstyle with unique upgrades and power-ups.

Diverse Enemies

Battle against a wide variety of monsters, each with their own attack patterns and behaviors.
